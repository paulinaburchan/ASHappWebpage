The Story

Built by a Passionate Language Learner

ASHapp was born from my own frustration as a language learner who couldn't find an app that truly understood the power of mnemonic techniques. I wanted something that could leverage AI to create a custom, fast-paced learning experience.

That's why I built ASHapp — not just another flashcard app, but a simple and incredibly helpful tool that combines proven mnemonic techniques with AI to help you learn faster than ever. The weirder the association, the better your brain remembers it!

The app grows every day, and many more features are coming. Can't wait to share them with you!

Features

Everything You Need to Remember More

Smart flashcards with AI-powered mnemonics, visual associations, and spaced repetition — all in one app.

Personal Goals

Set a learning goal that fits your schedule — per day, week, or month. Adjust anytime. The app adapts to your pace, never making you feel overwhelmed.

Collections

Organise concepts into collections for any subject or language. Don't want to start from scratch? Let AI generate a full collection for you in seconds.

Mnemonics Boost

Our AI visualizes your mental associations to make concepts stick like never before. Bizarre, vivid, unforgettable — that's how your brain loves to learn.

Smart Flashcard

Each flashcard is enriched with AI-generated context, example sentences, synonyms, and antonyms. The system adapts to your performance for optimal retention.

Daily Streak

Watch your streak grow day by day. Gentle reminders keep you on track without pressure — every small win is celebrated along the way.

Share Collections

Share your collections with friends, classmates, or teammates. Learn together, challenge each other, and build a community of knowledge seekers.

Smart OCR Import

Photograph your notes and watch the magic happen. ASHapp's AI converts entire pages into multiple flashcard concepts in seconds — not one word at a time like other apps.

Multilingual Learning

Master multiple languages simultaneously. Switch between languages effortlessly and build a truly polyglot learning experience with no limits.

Smart Writing Mode

Practice writing with intelligent fuzzy matching — minor typos won't count against you. Build confidence naturally while the algorithm focuses on real understanding.
