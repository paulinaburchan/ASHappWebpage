The Story

Built by a Passionate Language Learner

ASHapp was born from my own frustration as a language learner who couldn't find an app that truly understood the power of mnemonic techniques. I wanted something that could leverage AI to create a custom, fast-paced learning experience.

That's why I built ASHapp — not just another flashcard app, but a simple and incredibly helpful tool that combines proven mnemonic techniques with AI to help you learn faster than ever. The weirder the association, the better your brain remembers it!

The app grows every day, and many more features are coming. Can't wait to share them with you!

Features

Everything You Need to Remember More

Smart flashcards with AI-powered mnemonics, visual associations, and spaced repetition — all in one app.

Mnemonics Boost

Our AI visualizes your mental associations to make concepts stick like never before. Bizarre, vivid, unforgettable — that's how your brain loves to learn.

AI Concept Generation

Don't build your collection from scratch — let AI do the heavy lifting. Describe a topic and ASHapp generates a ready-to-study set of concepts with definitions, examples, and context in seconds.

Smart OCR Import

Photograph your notes and watch the magic happen. ASHapp's AI converts entire pages into multiple flashcard concepts in seconds — not one word at a time like other apps.

Flashcard Learning Mode

Each flashcard is enriched with AI-generated context, example sentences, synonyms, and antonyms — giving you everything you need to understand a concept, not just memorise it.

Typing Mode

Practice writing with intelligent fuzzy matching — minor typos won't count against you. Build confidence naturally while the algorithm focuses on real understanding.

Multilingual Learning

Master multiple languages simultaneously. Switch between languages effortlessly and build a truly polyglot learning experience with no limits.

Everything you need to know before you start learning.

Unlike Anki, ASHapp uses AI to automatically generate visual mnemonic images for each concept — turning abstract ideas into vivid, memorable pictures. It also generates full flashcard collections from a topic description, imports handwritten notes via OCR, and enriches every card with AI-written context, example sentences, synonyms, and antonyms. Anki is a blank canvas; ASHapp does the heavy lifting for you.
When you tap the mnemonic button on a flashcard, ASHapp's AI analyses the concept and generates a bizarre, vivid image that visually encodes a memory association. The image is unique to your concept and designed to exploit the brain's preference for unusual visuals over plain text. Images are generated via DALL-E 3 and stored in your account.

A mnemonic is a mental trick — often a vivid image, story, or association — that links new information to something already in your memory. Research shows mnemonic techniques can improve recall by up to 72% compared to plain repetition, because the human brain encodes visual and emotional information far more reliably than abstract text.
