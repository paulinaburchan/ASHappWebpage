Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Below is a concise web version; the in-app policy controls in case of differences.

Camera and Screenshots

Purpose: We only use the camera or accept screenshots when you report an issue in the app, so you can attach a screenshot to help us diagnose the problem. We do not perform background capture or biometric/face recognition.

Data Collected: The screenshot or image you choose to attach and any optional text you provide in the report.

Storage: Images remain on your device unless you explicitly submit them with the report. If submitted, they are stored securely on our servers for support purposes only.

Sharing: Not sold. Not shared with third parties except trusted processors (e.g., cloud storage/helpdesk) under data processing agreements.

Retention: Kept only until the reported issue is resolved or until you delete your account—whichever happens first.

Optional: Granting camera permission or attaching screenshots is optional. The app works without this permission; you can deny it and still use ASHapp.

Contact: privacy@ashapp.pl
