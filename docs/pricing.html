Frequently Asked Questions

OCR (Optical Character Recognition) lets you photograph pages of notes, textbooks, or handwritten vocabulary lists. ASHapp's AI scans the image and automatically creates multiple flashcard concepts from the text — not just one word at a time like other apps. It's exclusive to the Pioneer plan. Images are temporarily processed by Microsoft Azure Document Intelligence and are not stored after text extraction.
Instead of manually adding every concept, you can ask the AI to generate a whole collection for you on any topic — vocabulary sets, grammar concepts, historical facts, etc. Achiever gets 3 requests per month (up to 100 concepts per request), while Pioneer gets 100 requests per month (up to 300 concepts each). Content is generated via GPT-4 and filtered for safety and relevance.
AI Context enriches a flashcard with example sentences, usage notes, and explanations to help you understand a concept in context — not just its definition. AI Related Words shows synonyms, antonyms, and semantically similar words to deepen your understanding and expand your vocabulary network. Both are monthly request limits that reset each calendar month.
Paid plans (Achiever and Pioneer) will launch after the beta period. We will announce pricing before the public launch. Beta testers will receive exclusive early-bird discounts as a thank-you for helping shape the app. Payment will be available in all European currencies via PayPal, with monthly auto-renewal and support for promo codes.
Yes! You can change your subscription at any time from within the app under Profile / Account Settings. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period. The Starter plan is always available as the default minimum — you can never be left with nothing.
Each time you ask the AI to generate a visual mnemonic image for a flashcard concept, one credit is used. Starter users get 3 images as a one-time lifetime allowance to try the feature. Achiever and Pioneer users receive monthly credits that reset on the first of each month. Images are generated via DALL-E 3 and stored in your account on our secure AWS infrastructure.
If you downgrade, you keep all data you've created — you simply lose access to features above your new plan's limits. If you delete your account, all data is permanently and immediately deleted from our systems. Only anonymised system logs (no personal data) are retained for up to 90 days for technical debugging purposes.
